About HAVA

Hi, I'm Amber Havenaar — the photographer behind HAVA. Based in the Netherlands, I specialize in capturing the raw energy of live music, the personality of artists, and the atmosphere of events.

From Lowlands Festival stages to intimate club nights at Jimmy Woo, I've been trusted to document moments that matter. My approach is simple: be present, stay invisible, and let the moment speak.

Whether you're an artist looking for press photos, a venue needing event coverage, or a brand wanting authentic concert content — I'd love to work with you.
